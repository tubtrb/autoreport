# Privacy

This page explains the current privacy posture of the public Autoreport site at `auto-report.org`.

## Current site operation

The public site publishes product pages, guides, release notes, and development logs. The hosted demo is linked from the site but runs as a separate live service. Standard hosting, platform, and network logs may be used to keep the public pages available and to understand basic site health.

## Cookies and advertising

This content repository does not add live advertising code or consent tooling as part of the current public release workflow. If Autoreport later enables Google AdSense or other advertising products, this page will be updated and the site will add the required consent and disclosure flow before ads are served.

Google provides additional information about data use on partner sites here:

- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

## Analytics and diagnostics

Autoreport may rely on hosting-provider diagnostics, platform statistics, and ordinary web request logs to operate the site, investigate errors, and understand whether public pages are loading correctly. This site does not publish a broader custom analytics policy from this repository today.

## Third-party links and media

Public pages may link to:

- the hosted demo service
- the public GitHub repository
- external documentation such as Google policy pages

When you open a third-party link, that destination may collect information under its own privacy policy. Images and media embedded on the public site are published as part of the site content or linked from third-party platforms when needed for documentation.

## Future updates

If Autoreport changes its ad, analytics, cookie, or consent behavior in a user-visible way, this page will be updated together with the public site rollout.
